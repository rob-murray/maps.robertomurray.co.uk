Mapping #OpFortress

Mapping #operation fortress

This map visualises Tweets from @HantsPolice about #OpFortress, the operation to reduce crime in Hampshire. It has taken all Tweets containing road and place names and geolocated the Tweet against Ordnance Survey OS Locator and 1:50K Gazetteer data.

BBC Price of Football 2012

Mapping BBC Price of Football 2012

This map visualises the data collected by the BBC Price of Football survey 2012. The study looked at prices for 166 clubs in 10 divisions across British football, including the Conference Premier and Women's Super League.

BBC Secret Life of the Cat

BBC Secret Life of the Cat 2013

This map displays the GPS traces of the cats tracked during BBC's Horizon programme, entitled The Secret Life of the Cat. The programme had a website with the GPS traces but yet again the BBC don't really understand opendata - thanks to Rich Wareham who has reverse-engineered the GPS traces from the BBC's interactive map. I have simply taken his data and displayed it on some Ordnance Survey mapping.

With mapping provided by Ordnance Survey
